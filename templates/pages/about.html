Why DevGrade Exists

I built DevGrade after watching friends and classmates struggle with the same problem every semester: finding a final year project that was actually functional, well-documented, and ready to deploy. Most GitHub repos are half-finished tutorials. Most freelancers charge way too much for mediocre work.

I am a software engineer with years of experience building real production systems — LMS platforms, clinic management tools, veterinary SaaS, and university utilities. Every project on DevGrade is something I would be proud to submit myself.
